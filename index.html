Slime Guy

press space to start or pause

A, D or ← → to move

After committing countless horrendous crimes against humanity, Slime Guy was banished to the deepest darkest depths of Hell. It is up to the player to decide if Slime Guy survives and redeems himself, otherwise Slime Guy more like Slime Die.

Shoot demons for 10 points, be careful though, letting one escape loses you 20 points.

This game was built with responsiveness in mind, if you find it to be too difficult or too easy you can adjust the game size. (The smaller the screen, the harder it is.)
